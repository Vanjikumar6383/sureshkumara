Research Focus

Areas of Expertise

Interdisciplinary research spanning foundational AI to applied systems, with a focus on real-world impact.

Data Mining

Extracting actionable knowledge from large-scale structured and unstructured datasets using advanced pattern recognition and association rule learning.

Machine Learning

Developing supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning models for classification, regression, and intelligent decision-making systems.

Artificial Intelligence

Research in AI-driven prediction systems, knowledge representation, and intelligent automation across healthcare, education, and smart city domains.

Big Data Analytics

Scalable analytics pipelines for processing petabyte-scale data, with applications in climate modeling, IoT sensor networks, and urban systems.

Deep Learning

CNN, BiLSTM, Capsule Networks, and Transformer architectures for computer vision, NLP, and biomedical signal processing tasks.

Graph Neural Networks

GNN-based models for relational data, social network analysis, molecular property prediction, and knowledge graph reasoning.
